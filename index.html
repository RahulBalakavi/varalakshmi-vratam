Preparation

What is the Varalakshmi Vratam

  • A worship of Goddess Varalakshmi — Lakshmi who grants boons — for prosperity, progeny and the wellbeing of the whole family.
  • Kept mainly by married women on the Friday before Shravana Purnima (the full moon of Shravana month).
  • The Goddess is invoked into a decorated kalasham (pot), which is worshipped as Her very form.
  • A sacred yellow thread, the toram, is tied on the wrist as Her blessing and protection.
Talk-track — The Varalakshmi Vratam comes from the Skanda Purana, where Lord Shiva tells Parvati of a vratam that grants women every prosperity and saubhagya. It is one of the most beloved observances of Shravana month. Worshipping Varalakshmi is held equal to worshipping the eight Lakshmis together — Sri (wealth), Bhu (earth), Santana (progeny), and all the rest.

Preparation

Setup — The Kalasham as the Goddess

  • Clean the puja area; draw a lotus or muggu (rangoli) and place a plate of rice on it.
  • Set a Kalasham (pot) on the rice: fill with water (and a coin, turmeric, akshata), ring its rim with mango leaves, top with a coconut.
  • Dress the kalasham as the Goddess — wrap a cloth/blouse-piece, adorn with turmeric, kumkum, flowers, and a face of Lakshmi (a small mask or a turmeric mound).
  • Keep ready: the toram thread (yellow, turmeric-smeared), flowers, tulasi, panchamrutham, fruits, sweets, haldi-kumkum, bangles, incense and lamp.
  • Invite married women (muthaiduvas) and family to share the worship and the prasadam.
Talk-track — The heart of this Vratam is the kalasham, decorated and worshipped as Varalakshmi Herself. Take care making it beautiful — She is being welcomed as an honored guest and as the mother of the house. The toram, a yellow thread, is prepared with nine strands; it will be worshipped alongside the Goddess and then tied on the wrist.

Main Worship

Toram — The Sacred Thread

  • Take the toram — a yellow thread of nine strands, smeared with turmeric and tied with nine knots, adorned with a flower.
  • Worship it at the feet of the Goddess first, then tie it on your right wrist reciting the mantra.
  • Married women tie it on one another; it is Her blessing and protection for the year.

Talk-track — The toram is the signature of this Vratam — nine strands and nine knots of turmeric-yellow thread. First place it before the Goddess and worship it as part of Her, then tie it on your right wrist: 'I bind on my right hand this auspicious nine-fold thread; O Rama (Lakshmi), grant me the growth of children and grandchildren, and saubhagya.' A second traditional verse (Navatantu-samaayuktam…) is also used; either is correct.

Offerings

Dhupam · Deepam · Naivedyam

  • Dhupam — offer incense · Deepam — show the lamp.
  • Naivedyam — offer the prasadam: payasam, sweets, fruits, jaggery, and the traditional offerings.
  • Offer with the prana-ahuti and a sprinkle of water around the food.

Offerings

Mantrapushpam · Neeraajanam · Pradakshina

  • Thaamboolam — betel · Neeraajanam — camphor aarti.
  • Mantrapushpam — recite the Sri Suktam, the great Vedic hymn to Lakshmi, and the Varalakshmi flower-shlokas.
  • Offer a handful of flowers at Her feet, then Pradakshina Namaskaaram — circle and prostrate.

The Story

The Vrata Katha — Charumathi

  • Parvati asks Shiva for a vratam that grants women prosperity and saubhagya; Shiva tells of Charumathi.
  • Charumathi, a devoted wife of Kundinapuram, is visited in a dream by Goddess Varalakshmi, who teaches her this Vratam.
  • She and the town's women perform it on the Shravana Friday; their homes fill with gold, gems and grain, and their families flourish.
  • From then, women have kept this Vratam every year — read the full story from a Katha book, and chant the Goddess's name.

చాలా కాలం క్రితం, పార్వతి దేవి తన కుటుంబాల సంక్షేమం మరియు శ్రేయస్సు కోసం మహిళలు చేయవలసిన వ్రతం గురించి శివుడిని కోరింది.
Long ago, the goddess Parvati asked Lord Shiva for a vratam that women might perform for the welfare and prosperity of their families.
శివుడు వరలక్ష్మి వ్రతాన్ని అన్నింటిలో అత్యంత ప్రయోజనకరమైనదిగా పేర్కొని, చారుమతి కథ గురించి ఆమెకు చెప్పాడు.
Shiva named the Varalakshmi Vratam as the most beneficial of all, and told her the story of Charumathi.
చారుమతి విదర్భ దేశంలోని కుండిన పట్టణానికి భక్తిగల, అంకితభావంగల భార్య, ఆమె స్వచ్ఛమైన హృదయంతో తన గృహాన్ని చూసుకుంటూ, లక్ష్మీ దేవిని పూజించేది.
Charumathi was a pious and devoted wife of the town of Kundina in the Vidarbha country, who served her household with a pure heart and worshipped the goddess Lakshmi.
ఆమె భక్తి చూసి సంతోషించిన దేవత వరలక్ష్మి ఆమెకు కలలో ప్రత్యక్షమైంది.
Pleased by her devotion, the goddess Varalakshmi appeared to her in a dream.
ఆమె చారుమతి యొక్క భక్తికి ప్రశంసించి, ఆమెకు వరలక్ష్మి వ్రతాన్ని చేయమని చెప్పింది, దీని ద్వారా ఆమె కోరికలన్నీ నెరవేరుతాయి.
She praised Charumathi's piety and told her to perform the Varalakshmi Vratam, by which all her wishes would be fulfilled.
శ్రావణ మాసంలో పౌర్ణమికి ముందు శుక్రవారం నాడు దీనిని పాటించమని ఆ దేవత ఆమెకు సూచించింది, మొత్తం ఆరాధనను ఆమెకు నేర్పింది.
The goddess instructed her to observe it on the Friday before the full moon in the month of Shravana, and taught her the whole worship.
చారుమతి ఆనందంతో మేల్కొని తన భర్తకు, కుటుంబానికి ఆ కల గురించి చెప్పి, దానిని ప్రదర్శించమని ఆమెను ప్రోత్సహించారు.
Charumathi woke with joy and told her husband and family of the dream, and they encouraged her to perform it.
వార్త పట్టణం అంతటా వ్యాపించింది, మరియు వివాహిత మహిళలు ఆమెతో కలిసి వ్రతాన్ని కొనసాగించడానికి ఆసక్తిగా గుమిగూడారు.
The news spread through the town, and the married women gathered eagerly to keep the vratam together with her.
నిర్ణీత శ్రావణ శుక్రవారం నాడు వారు కలశాన్ని ఏర్పాటు చేసి, లోతైన భక్తితో వరలక్ష్మిని పూజించారు.
On the appointed Shravana Friday they set up the kalasham and worshipped Varalakshmi with deep devotion.
చారుమతి ఆ దేవత చుట్టూ తన భక్తి వృత్తాకారంలో నడుస్తుండగా, వరలక్ష్మి యొక్క దయ ఆమె శరీరంపై కనిపించింది: ఆమె పాదాలపై బంగారు చెప్పులు మెరిసిపోయాయి, తొమ్మిది రత్నాల కంకణాలు ఆమె చేతులను అలంకరించాయి.
As Charumathi walked her circles of reverence around the goddess, the grace of Varalakshmi appeared upon her very body: golden anklets shone on her feet, and bracelets of nine gems adorned her arms.
చివరి రౌండ్లో ఆమె మరియు అన్ని మహిళలు ఆభరణాలతో అలంకరించబడ్డారు, మరియు వారి ఇళ్ళు ధాన్యం, ఆభరణాలు మరియు బంగారంతో నిండిపోయాయి.
With the final round she and all the women were decked with ornaments, and their homes overflowed with grain, jewels, and gold.
మహిళలు ఆ దేవత బహుమతిని పంచుకున్నందుకు చారుమతికి ఆశీర్వదించారు, ఆ రోజు నుండి మహిళలు తమ కుటుంబాల శ్రేయస్సు కోసం ప్రతి సంవత్సరం వరంలక్ష్మి వ్రతాన్ని పాటిస్తున్నారు.
The women blessed Charumathi for sharing the goddess's gift, and from that day women have kept the Varalakshmi Vratam every year for the wellbeing of their families.

Conclusion

Kshamapana · Theertham · Udvasanam

  • Kshamapana — ask the Goddess's forgiveness for any error or lack in the worship.
  • Take Theertham — the holy water; offer the final Maha Naivedyam.
  • Udvasanam — respectfully conclude, asking the Goddess to remain gracious in the home.
  • Give the muthaiduvas haldi-kumkum, bangles, a coconut and prasadam; share the blessing with all.
